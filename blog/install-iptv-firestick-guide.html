How to Set Up IPTV on Amazon Firestick
(2025 Guide)

UltraStreamNow Team Nov 23, 2025 5 min read

Looking to unlock thousands of live TV channels and on-demand movies on your Amazon Firestick? You're in the right place. In this guide, we'll show you exactly how to set up IPTV on your Firestick in under 5 minutes.

Amazon Firestick is the most popular device for IPTV streaming due to its affordability, ease of use, and ability to sideload apps. Whether you have the Fire TV Stick 4K, Fire TV Stick Lite, or the Fire TV Cube, this guide works for all models.

Prerequisites

Before we begin, make sure you have the following:

  • An Amazon Firestick connected to your TV and internet.
  • An active IPTV subscription (like UltraStreamNow).
  • Your IPTV login details (M3U URL or Xtream Codes).

Step 1: Prepare Your Firestick for Installation

Since IPTV apps aren't usually available in the official Amazon App Store, we need to enable "Apps from Unknown Sources" to install them. This is perfectly safe.

  1. From the Firestick Home screen, go to Settings (gear icon).
  2. Select My Fire TV.
  3. Choose Developer Options. (Note: If you don't see Developer Options, click on "About" and then click on "Fire TV Stick" 7 times to unlock it.)
  4. Turn ON Apps from Unknown Sources.

Step 2: Install the Downloader App

We'll use the "Downloader" app to get the IPTV player. It's a free official app.

  1. Go back to the Home screen and click Find (magnifying glass).
  2. Search for "Downloader".
  3. Select the orange Downloader app icon.
  4. Click Download or Get to install it.

Step 3: Install IPTV Smarters Pro (Recommended Player)

IPTV Smarters Pro is one of the best and most user-friendly IPTV players available.

  1. Open the Downloader app.
  2. In the URL box, type this code: 78522 (or enter https://www.iptvsmarters.com/smarters.apk).
  3. Click Go. The file will start downloading.
  4. Once downloaded, click Install.
  5. After installation, click Open.

Step 4: Log in to Your IPTV Service

Now that you have the player installed, it's time to connect your service.

  1. Open IPTV Smarters Pro.
  2. Select Login with Xtream Codes API (this is the easiest method).
  3. Enter the details provided by your IPTV provider:
    • Any Name: Enter "UltraStreamNow" (or any name you like).
    • Username: Your username.
    • Password: Your password.
    • URL: The server URL provided in your welcome email.
  4. Click Add User.

Wait a few seconds for the channels, movies, and series to load. Congratulations! You are now ready to stream.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Buffering or Freezing?

If you experience buffering, try these quick fixes:

  • Check your internet speed: You need at least 25 Mbps for smooth HD streaming.
  • Restart your router: Unplug it for 30 seconds and plug it back in.
  • Use a VPN: Sometimes ISPs throttle streaming traffic. A VPN can prevent this.
