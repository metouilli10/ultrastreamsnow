## CONTENT OPTIMIZATION FORMULA

For each target keyword, use this formula:

### On-Page Optimization
1. **Title Tag:** Include keyword in first 60 characters
2. **H1:** Primary keyword variation
3. **First Paragraph:** Keyword within first 100 words
4. **H2s:** Include keyword variations
5. **Body Content:** Natural keyword density 1-2%
6. **Image Alt Text:** Keyword in at least one image
7. **URL:** Include keyword (if new page)
8. **Meta Description:** Compelling copy with keyword

### Content Length Guidelines
- **Homepage:** 800-1,200 words
- **Service Pages:** 1,000-1,500 words
- **Blog Posts:** 1,500-2,500 words
- **Ultimate Guides:** 3,000-5,000 words
- **Location Pages:** 800-1,200 words

### Internal Linking
- Link to pages with target keyword as anchor text
- 3-5 contextual internal links per page
- Link from high-authority pages to new content
